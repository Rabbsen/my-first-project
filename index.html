About our team

Who are we?
well we are a small Catch and Release fishing team grounded in 2016 with a main focus on Northen pike fishing, we are based in the south of Sweden and fish all over the country and sometimes abroad. We are a team of 4 anglers that loves to fish and compete in tournaments, we also love to share our passion for fishing with others. Our main goal is to promote Catch and Release fishing and to inspire others to do the same. We also want to show that fishing is not only about catching fish, but also about enjoying nature and spending time with friends. So if you are interested in fishing, nature and having fun, join us on our journey!
